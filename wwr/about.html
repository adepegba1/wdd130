Adepegba Rafting Organisation

At Adepegba's, our purpose is to connect people with the wild beauty of nature through unforgettable whitewater rafting experiences. We believe the river is not just a place for adventure—it’s a path to personal growth, teamwork, and lasting memories.

History

Adepegba Rafting Organisation was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate river guides and outdoor enthusiasts who believed that the thrill of whitewater rafting should be more than just an adrenaline rush—it should be a meaningful, shared experience with nature and people.

What began as a small outfit with a single raft and a big dream along the Lagos,Nigeria quickly grew into one of the region’s most trusted rafting companies. Our founders, all certified rafting professionals with decades of combined experience, built the business on three core values: safety, connection, and respect for the river.

Over the years, we’ve guided thousands of adventurers—families, friends, solo explorers, and corporate teams—through some of the most scenic and challenging whitewater routes. Each season brought new memories, deeper community ties, and a growing team of passionate guides who share our mission.

Today, Adepegba Rafting Organisation continues to thrive as a locally owned and operated business committed to excellence in outdoor recreation. We’ve expanded our offerings to include not only half-day and full-day rafting trips, but also multi-day wilderness expeditions, eco-education tours, and youth adventure camps.

Despite our growth, one thing has never changed: our love for the river and our commitment to creating unforgettable, safe, and empowering e xperiences for every guest.

Join us, and become part of the Adepegba Rafting Organisation legacy—where the river writes the stories, and you become part of the adventure.
